This activity aims for students to explore a historical period through direct observation of the textbook.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assign a historical period">
    1. Each group or student must work on a specific period from the book (for example, Paleolithic, Neolithic, Age of Metals...).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data search">
    Students must look in their book for the assigned period and search for data to fill in the Historical Data table.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Final Historical Data">
    As they read, they should note information in the following categories:

    - **Technology**: What tools, techniques, or inventions were used in that period?
    - **Environment**: What was the natural environment where they lived like? What climate, flora, and fauna predominated?
    - **Society**: How were they socially organized? What customs, beliefs, or types of housing did they have?
    - **Key Innovation**: What important advance stands out in that stage?

      ![Datoshistoricos(1) Pn](/images/datoshistoricos\(1\).png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Recommendations:**

<Info>
  - Encourage them to prioritize information from the book before resorting to other sources.
  - Encourage them to write in their own words to ensure understanding.
  - Remind them that each row of the table must be consistent with the assigned period.
</Info>
